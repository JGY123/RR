# Email Report Generator

## What it does
"Email" button in header generates a formatted HTML email body with the strategy summary, ready to paste into Outlook/Gmail. Pre-formatted for institutional communication.

## Email template structure:

Subject line: "[Strategy] Risk Report — Week ending [Date]"

Body:
```
[STRATEGY NAME] — Risk Summary
Week ending April 7, 2026 | Benchmark: MSCI EAFE

RISK PROFILE
• Tracking Error: 5.69% (↑0.14 vs prior week)
• Active Share: 88.8% | Beta: 1.04 | Holdings: 44
• Risk Split: 65% Stock-Specific / 35% Factor-Driven
• Cash: 4.2%

KEY CHANGES THIS WEEK
• [Auto-generated from exec summary bullets]

TOP SECTOR BETS
Industrials: +6.6% OW (25.1% of TE)
Health Care: +5.2% OW (11.1% of TE)
Financials: -4.9% UW (10.3% of TE)

TOP RISK CONTRIBUTORS
1. Nebius Group (NBIS) — 16.3% of TE, Q5 rank
2. Advantest (6857) — 4.9% of TE, Q1 rank
3. Ascendis Pharma (ASND) — 3.8% of TE, Q5 rank

FACTOR PROFILE
Largest tilts: Growth +0.37, Volatility +0.28, Momentum +0.27
Largest reverse: Dividend Yield -0.57, Value -0.33

---
Generated by Redwood Risk Control Panel
```

## Implementation:
```javascript
function generateEmailBody(){
  let s = cs;
  let lines = [];
  lines.push(`${s.name || s.id} — Risk Summary`);
  lines.push(`Week ending ${isoDate(s.current_date)} | Benchmark: ${s.benchmark || s.id}`);
  lines.push('');
  lines.push('RISK PROFILE');
  lines.push(`• Tracking Error: ${f2(s.sum.te,2)}%`);
  // ... build full email
  
  // Copy to clipboard
  navigator.clipboard.writeText(lines.join('\n'));
  showToast('Email body copied to clipboard','var(--pos)');
}
```

Wire to the existing "Email" button in the header.
Also generate an HTML version with inline styles for rich email clients.
