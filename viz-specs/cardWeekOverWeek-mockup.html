Why this design wins: the PM gets four answers in 3 seconds — (1) did risk increase, (2) did the portfolio composition change materially, (3) where, and (4) which factor bets shifted. Single-tile vertical scan. No tab-hopping. The 3 columns mirror how a PM thinks about turnover — added / dropped / resized — not how the data is shipped.
